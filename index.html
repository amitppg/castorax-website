About Simmi Enterprises

Your Trusted Partner in Castor Oil Supply Since 1998

Simmi Enterprises was founded in 1998 and has established itself as one of the leading suppliers of all kinds of castor oil in India. With over 25 years of consistent service to the pharmaceutical industry, we have built lasting relationships with major pharmaceutical companies.
